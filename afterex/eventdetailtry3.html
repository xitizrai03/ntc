About This Event

Join Nepal Telecom for an immersive workshop on 5G technology, where industry experts will guide you through the fundamentals and advanced concepts of next-generation telecommunications. This full-day event is designed for both technical and non-technical professionals who want to understand how 5G will transform Nepal's digital landscape.

Workshop Highlights

  • Understanding 5G architecture and spectrum
  • Case studies from global 5G implementations
  • Hands-on demonstration of 5G equipment
  • Network security considerations
  • Roadmap for 5G rollout in Nepal

Who Should Attend?

Professionals

Telecom engineers, network architects, and IT professionals looking to upgrade their skills.

Students

Engineering students interested in telecommunications careers.
