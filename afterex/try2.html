About Nepal Telecom

Pioneering Nepal's telecommunications since 2032 BS

Nepal Telecom (NTC) is the leading and largest telecommunications service provider in Nepal. As a state-owned enterprise, we are committed to connecting every Nepali household with reliable and affordable communication services.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide in Nepal by expanding our network coverage to remote areas while continuously upgrading our technology to meet international standards.
